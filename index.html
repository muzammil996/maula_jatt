The legend of Maula Jatt

The Legend of Maula Jatt is a 2022 Pakistani Punjabi-language action drama film directed by Bilal Lashari. It is a reboot of the 1979 cult classic Maula Jatt, which was directed by Younis Malik and starred Sultan Rahi in the titular role. The film features Fawad Khan as Maula Jatt and Hamza Ali Abbasi as Noori Natt, with Mahira Khan, Humaima Malick, and others in supporting roles.

The story revolves around the rivalry between Maula Jatt, a heroic figure who fights against injustice, and Noori Natt, a ruthless antagonist. The film explores themes of honor, revenge, and the struggle for justice in a rural setting.

The Legend of Maula Jatt received critical acclaim for its direction, performances, action sequences, and production values. It was praised for revitalizing the Pakistani film industry and bringing Punjabi cinema to a wider audience. The film also achieved commercial success at the box office.

Overall, The Legend of Maula Jatt is considered a significant milestone in Pakistani cinema and has garnered a dedicated fan base both domestically and internationally.
